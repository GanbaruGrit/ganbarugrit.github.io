Environmental Design

What it is:

Various different environmental designs that showcase the ability to create everything from dark brooding dungeons to bright outdoor areas with rich moving foliage and dynamic lighting.

These environments were specifically created to integrate seamlessly with the Unreal Engine's open-world capibilities. This allows them to be used for procedural generation, live scaling, and culling.
