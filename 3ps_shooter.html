3rd Person Shooter - Animation Blending Prototype

Custom animation blending module created for use with artists in mind. Applied to a 3rd person shooter to demonstrate free form movement.

What it is:

An animation blending system built using a combination of C++ and Blueprints. It was built with ease of us in mind as a potential tool for collaborating with artists. It's compatible with all commonly used skeleton meshes and rigged animations with a large array of configurable options exposed through Blueprints allowing quick and easy changes to be made.

Shown here is it being applied to 3rd person shooter prototype to demonstrate how the eight way walk seamlessly transitions to different directions while handling various walk speeds.

View the full video here

Engine: Unreal Engine 5.2
Platform: PC, Mac, WebGL

Designed For Collaboratin

Robust Blueprint system used so that animations can easily be configured in-editor.

8-Way Blending

Multiple animation cycles being blended seamlessly.

Multiple Movement Type Support

Animations adapt to different walk speeds such as transitioning from a walk to run.

Tracking and Turning

Model animation will smoothly track and turn with player or other desired targets.
